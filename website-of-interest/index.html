The Author



J.K. Rowling

J.K. Rowling is the British author who created the magical world of Harry Potter . She first imagined the story during a train journey in 1990 and published the first book in 1997 . The Harry Potter series became one of the most successful book series in history,inspiring movies, games, and millions of fans worldwide.

Harry Potter and the Philispher's Stone

A young orphan named Harry Potter discovers on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. There, he makes friends, learns magic, and uncovers the mystery of the Philosopher’s Stone—an object that grants immortality—while confronting the dark past connected to Lord Voldemort.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

In his second year at Hogwarts, Harry hears a mysterious voice that no one else can hear, while students begin turning to stone one by one. Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

With Voldemort’s return denied by the Ministry of Magic, Harry is isolated and discredited. At Hogwarts, a strict new authority figure takes control, pushing Harry and his friends to secretly train in defensive magic. As visions connect Harry’s mind to Voldemort, the battle between good and evil intensifies, culminating in a dangerous confrontation and a heartbreaking loss.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

Harry begins to uncover the history of Voldemort with Dumbledore’s guidance, learning about Horcruxes—the key to defeating him. At the same time, a mysterious potions book labeled “The Half-Blood Prince” helps Harry excel in class. As dark forces grow stronger outside Hogwarts, betrayal and tragedy strike, leading to one of the most emotional moments in the series.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hollows-Part 1

After Harry Potter learns he must destroy pieces of Lord Voldemort’s soul (Horcruxes), he sets out with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. Along the way, they discover the mysterious Deathly Hallows—three powerful magical objects tied to mastering death—while their mission becomes increasingly dangerous and uncertain.

Character

Harry Potter

Student of Gryffindor.Harry Potter is a brave young wizard with a lightning-shaped scar. He is known for his courage, loyalty, and strong sense of justice as he faces challenges and protects his friends from danger.

Draco Malfoy

Student of Slytherin.Draco Malfoy is known as an arrogant, wealthy bully, and the son of a Death Eater. He frequently antagonizes Harry, relying on his pure-blood status.

Luna Lovegood

Student of Ravenclaw.Luna Lovegood is an eccentric, dreamy Ravenclaw student known for her unwavering authenticity, "dottiness," and unexpected wisdomloyal,friend to Harry Potter and member of Dumbledore's ArmyHarry.

Cedric Diggory

Student of Hufflepuff. As a sixth-year Prefect and Quidditch Captain ,Cedric Diggory is a popular Hogwarts champion in the Triwizard Tournamentbefore his tragic death.
